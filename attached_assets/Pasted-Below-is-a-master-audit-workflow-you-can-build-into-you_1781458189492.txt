Below is a master audit workflow you can build into your GRC platform and reuse across ISO 27001, SOC 2, RBI, SEBI, PCI DSS, NIST CSF, DPDPA/GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 42001.

Universal Compliance Audit Workflow
Stage	Workflow	Output
1	Select framework and audit type	ISO audit, SOC 2 audit, RBI audit, SEBI audit, etc.
2	Define scope	Entity, location, process, application, cloud, vendor, data, AI systems
3	Create audit plan	Audit objective, criteria, dates, auditor, auditee, sampling method
4	Map requirements to controls	Framework control library
5	Assign control owners	RACI matrix
6	Perform pre-audit questionnaire	Readiness score
7	Collect documents	Policy, SOP, registers, reports, logs
8	Collect technical evidence	IAM, MFA, EDR, SIEM, VA, backup, cloud, DLP evidence
9	Conduct walkthrough	Process understanding
10	Test design effectiveness	Is the control properly designed?
11	Test operating effectiveness	Is the control working consistently?
12	Record observations	Compliant / partially compliant / non-compliant
13	Rate findings	Critical, high, medium, low
14	Create CAPA	Corrective and preventive action plan
15	Validate remediation	Evidence-based closure
16	Conduct management review	Leadership approval
17	Generate audit report	Final audit report
18	Certification / regulator readiness	Audit pack
19	Continuous monitoring	Compliance dashboard

SOC 2 audits are based on AICPA Trust Services Criteria covering Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, and Privacy. SEBI issued its Cybersecurity and Cyber Resilience Framework for regulated entities in August 2024, with later FAQs/clarifications in 2025. RBI’s IT Governance, Risk, Controls and Assurance directions are important for Indian regulated entities.

6. NIST CSF Audit Workflow

NIST CSF 2.0 is structured around Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.

Function	Audit Focus
Govern	Policy, risk governance, roles, oversight
Identify	Assets, risks, vendors, business context
Protect	Access control, awareness, data security
Detect	Monitoring, SIEM, anomaly detection
Respond	Incident response, communication
Recover	Backup, DR, restoration

Current profile
Target profile

Maturity score
